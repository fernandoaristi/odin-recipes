Steak

Above, I present a perfect medium rare ribeye steak

This recipe is very fast to make for individuals low on time, like developers.

Usually, in a total of 10 minutes, the entire dish should be finished.

Ingredients

  • 12oz Ribeye
  • Salt and pepper
  • Baby tomatoes
  • Asparagus

Steps

  1. Grab 2 separate non-stick pans and place one on high heat and the other on low heat.
  2. Season the steak and vegetables generously with salt & pepper
  3. Place steak on high heat pan for 3 minutes per side
  4. Now put vegetables on low heat pan and allow them to draw out their moisture and get sauteed. Normally 5 minutes should be enough.
  5. After the steak finishes it 6 minutes cooking, remove it from the pan and let it rest for 4 minutes.
  6. Vegetables will be also done by then, you can turn off the heat and leave them in the pan until steak finishes resting.
  7. After the remaining 4 minutes, everything will be finished and ready to it. All in under 10 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
