High protein, healthy oatmeal recipe

Ingredients

  • Protein powder
  • Half of a banana
  • 4oz of cherries
  • 1 tbsp of low fat peanut butter
  • 1 cup of Quaker oatmeal
  • 1 cup of whole milk

    • Optional:

  • Cinnamon powder
  • 1/2 tsp of salt

Steps

  1. Measure 1 scoop of vanilla flavored protein
  2. Gather the 4oz of cherries and half a banana, and cut the banana into circles
  3. In a pot, put the cup of milk on a low simmer until it starts to slightly boil
  4. Add the raw oatmeal, keep the heal on very low and cover the pot for 10 minutes
  5. After 10 minutes, turn off the heat and allow 2-3 minutes for the oatmeal to cool inside the pan, which will make it easier to remove
  6. Serve oatmeal in a bowl, add your fruits cold or room temperature, and mix in the peanut butter. You may also add in your optional ingredients at this point.
  7. Enjoy!
