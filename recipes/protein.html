Protein Shake

Above is a beautiful picture of a well blended, and highly nutritious protein shake.

Ingredients

  • Protein Powder
  • Whole Milk
  • Nuts of choice
  • Fruits of choice

Steps

  1. Measure 2 full scoops of your favorite protein powder
  2. Measure 4oz of your fruits and nuts of choice
  3. Fill the blender bottle with up to 12fl oz of whole milk
  4. Add rest of ingredients in the blender, and blend for up to 20 seconds for a thin and fluid consistency, or up to 60 seconds for a thicker, foamy consistency.
  5. Enjoy!
